You have the guide. Now make the decision clearer.

Use it with one real decision in mind. The value is not in reading everything; it is in noticing which question you can no longer avoid.

If the guide has already put a specific vendor, AI use case, board conversation, or operating model question in your head, that is the thread worth pulling.

Start a conversation Use a Corbelle tool

Before you forward it, take ten minutes.

01

Name the decision.

Write down the decision as it stands today, not the project name. If it starts with a vendor or a tool, it is probably framed too late.

02

Separate pressure from evidence.

Deadlines, board questions, investor expectations and team fatigue are real. They are not the same thing as evidence that the proposed answer is right.

03

Decide who should be in the room.

The wrong people in the room makes the conversation political. The right people in the room makes it precise.

When the decision matters, clarity before commitment is not delay. It is leadership.
