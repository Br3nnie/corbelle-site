How it works

The product is the thinking loop.

Clarity Engine does not ask leaders to learn another dashboard before they get value. It meets them inside Cowork, where the decision is already being discussed.

01

Frame the decision

The skill separates the visible question from the pressure underneath it: politics, timing, confidence, unknowns, and reversibility.

02

Create the memo

Each conversation becomes a clean HTML decision memo with options, assumptions, trade-offs, signals to watch, and a recommended next move.

03

Store the record

The hosted service saves the memo and metadata against the subscriber account, so decision history is available across devices.

04

Review the outcome

Retrospectives are appended later, turning individual decisions into a pattern library rather than a pile of forgotten notes.
